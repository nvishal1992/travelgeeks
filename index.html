About Us

This TravelGeeks site is owned by Vishal Setty and Tribhuvan Shetty. We both are passionate about travelling to new places. Yes, many times we take our bikes with us too. We visited some places which name was not there on Google maps and thus helped Google to improve.
I (Vishal) have a hobby of photography and Tribhuvan is the one who plans out for all travelling stuff (very good at that). We came up with a plan to create some platform to share our travelling experience, so that it might help the people who are planning to visit those places.
There are many websites where we can get the information about places, but how we stand different is we share the experience of our visit to many destinations which are near to Bengaluru which will be the perfect weekend gateways.
